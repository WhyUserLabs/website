WhyUser Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 14, 2025

Welcome to WhyUser. This Privacy Policy explains how WhyUser, Inc. ("WhyUser," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you visit our website (www.whyuser.com) and any other websites, social media pages, or forms that link to this Privacy Policy (collectively, the "Sites").

1.0 Applicability of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected on our Sites.

This Privacy Policy does not apply to the "Customer Data" that we process on behalf of our customers (our "Design Partners" or "Customers") through our B2B SaaS platform (the "Service"). "Customer Data" includes the sensitive go-to-market materials, sales call transcripts, CRM data, and other information that our Customers upload to our Service for simulation and analysis.

Our collection, use, and processing of Customer Data is governed by the Beta User Agreement or other master agreement between us and our Customer. If you are an individual whose data has been submitted to our Service by a WhyUser Customer (such as an employee or customer of that Customer), you should contact that Customer directly with any privacy-related questions.

2.0 Information We Collect

We collect information in a few different ways:

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Request a demo or more information;
  • Sign up for our waitlist or newsletter;
  • Register for a webinar;
  • Contact us for support or any other reason.

This information may include your name, email address, job title, company name, phone number, and any other information you choose to provide in your message.

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you visit our Sites, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

  • Device and Log Information: We collect your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and information about your activity on the Sites.
  • Information from Cookies and Trackers: We and our service providers use cookies and similar technologies (like pixels and web beacons) to analyze trends, administer the Sites, track users' movements around the Sites, and gather demographic information. This Privacy Policy does not cover the use of cookies by third parties. You can control the use of cookies at the individual browser level.

3.0 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various business purposes, including to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Sites;
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and demo requests;
  • Communicate with you about products, services, and events offered by WhyUser, including marketing and promotional messages (you can opt-out at any time);
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Sites;
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities and protect the rights and property of WhyUser and others;
  • Comply with our legal and financial obligations.

4.0 How We Share Your Information

We may share your information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: We share information with third-party vendors and service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as website hosting, analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), and email delivery. These providers are authorized to use your information only as necessary to provide these services to us.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if we believe it's required by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or if we have a good faith belief that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, or investigate fraud.
  • In a Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, financing due diligence, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice on our Site of any change in ownership.
  • With Your Consent: We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

We do not sell your personal information.

5.0 Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect the information we collect. However, no electronic transmission or storage of data is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6.0 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, or as needed to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7.0 Your Data Rights

You may have certain rights regarding your personal information, depending on your location. This includes the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@whyuser.com.

7.1 Your California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). These rights include:

  • Right to Know: You have the right to request that we disclose what personal information we collect, use, and disclose.
  • Right to Delete: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not "sell" or "share" your personal information as those terms are defined by the CCPA.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

To exercise your California privacy rights, please contact us at privacy@whyuser.com.

California's "Shine the Light" law (Civil Code Section § 1798.83) permits users of our Sites that are California residents to request certain information regarding our disclosure of personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. To make such a request, please contact us at privacy@whyuser.com.

8.0 International Data Transfers

WhyUser, Inc. is based in the United States, and we process and store information in the U.S. If you are located outside the U.S., be aware that your information will be transferred to, processed, and stored in the U.S.

9.0 Children's Privacy

Our Sites are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

10.0 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.
