02 · The census plate

Read down the Economic Buyer column

One row per page, five readers across, the page's own score on the right. Read down rather than across: the column is the category's problem, the row is one company's. One column keeps going dark. The Economic Buyer advanced on 5 of 34 companies and was slowed or stopped on 29.

Across the census, 135 of 510 simulated runs ended in someone leaving the page, and 28 of 170 role readings ended that way.

03 · Named failure modes

Trust case complete, value case absent

The most common failure mode here is not a design problem or a copy problem, and it has a name. On 18 of 34 companies the arithmetic on the page facts says the trust case is made and the value case is not. The committee runs arrive at the same place independently: the Champion is armed and still cannot justify the spend, on 21 of 34.

15 · Method

How this was measured, and what it is not

There is no traffic in this report and there are no respondents. Every simulated finding is reported as runs: 510 of them, five committee roles across three behavioural states on each of 34 companies, one strategy directive per page. Counted facts and simulated reads are tagged separately throughout and never averaged together.

16 · Glossary

The vocabulary, so it survives the meeting

A named failure mode is something a colleague can repeat on Monday without you in the room. An unnamed one has to be re-derived every time it comes up, which is usually never.

Buying committee
The group of people who together decide whether a purchase happens. A page is not read by a company. It is read by five different jobs with different fears, and they have to hand the decision to each other.
Economic Buyer
The person whose budget it is. Cares about cost, payback, risk and whether the vendor still exists in three years. Strictest reader on the page.
Technical Decision Maker
The person who has to make it work. Cares about architecture, integrations and depth, and reads marketing language as noise.
Champion
The person inside the company who already wants this and has to sell it upward. Needs ammunition, not features.
End User
The person who will operate it daily. Cares about whether they can start without asking anyone.
Security Guardian
The person who can stop the deal on their own. Cares about certifications, data handling, and whether the claims can be verified.
Behavioural state
The same person reads differently depending on motivation and attention. Every role is run as a skeptic who assumes the page is marketing, a distracted visitor who sees only the top of the page, and an ideal visitor who explores properly.
Handoff
The moment one committee member has to pass the decision to another. Most pages are written for one reader and go silent at exactly this moment, which is why so many deals stall without anyone naming a reason.
Counted finding
A page fact any stranger can confirm in ten seconds. Whether pricing is public. How many quantified proof points sit above the fold. These aggregate into hard statements.
Simulated finding
What the committee did when it read the page. Role verdicts, broken handoffs, frictions and fixes. Directional, reported as runs, never presented as measured traffic.
Denominator
The number of companies a finding was actually assessable on. Every statement here carries one. A rate without a denominator is decoration, so a share is only ever shown next to the count it came from, and only where at least twenty companies could be assessed.
Anchor
The single page-observable fact that causes a broken handoff. An inference about people who are not in the room is a horoscope until it is anchored to something checkable. This report has no respondents and no quotes; the anchor is its unit of evidence.
Within noise
A difference the size chance produces at these numbers. Comparing enough things guarantees a few striking-looking results, so every comparison here was tested and the ones that did not survive are marked rather than deleted.
Census id
The handle a page carries on a chart, C07 rather than a name. It means nothing outside the records that produced it.
