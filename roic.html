iii.

Attribution controls

The variables a CFO objects to most. Exposed so you set them. Move to 40% / 40% for startup-pragmatic.

40%
Share of performance lift attributable to WhyUser (rest to sales, product, market). Default 40%.
40%
Conservative reduction on top of attribution. Higher = more conservative. Default 40%.
50%
Share of team throughput gain credited as GTM velocity. 50% splits the reclaimed hour between time savings and velocity.
45%
Share of budget waste diagnosable / preventable by WhyUser. Default 45%.
